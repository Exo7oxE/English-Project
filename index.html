Health is the amount
of damage you can 
take before dying.
Keep it above 0.
You can restore it 
with health potions.

Strength is what
determines how much
damage you can deal.
Weapons can add to
your base strength.

Defense is what
determines how much
damage will be
subtracted from the
enemy's attack. armor
can add to your base
defense and blocking
will increase defense
for one turn.

Stamina is what
determines how many
times you can attack
before you are tired.
You regain 1 stamina
per turn.

Goblins are small
creatures with
all around low stats.

Slimes are strange
bulbous balls of gel
that are rather weak
but are durable due
to their squishiness.

Skeletons are strong
all-rounders that
you should be
cautious of.

Haunted Knights are
suits of armor being
controlled by a
mysterious force.
They have high attack
and defense, but low
health and stamina.

Health potions refill
10 points of health.
Stamina potions refill
5 points of stamina.

Rage potions will
increase your
strength and raise
crit chance	for
the rest of 
the battle.

Stone potions will
increase your 
defense and make
fully blocked
attacks deal 0
damage for the
rest of the battle.

Smoke bombs will
increase the chance
for an enemy to miss
you for the rest of
the battle.

A stamina bracelet
will lower the
amount of stamina it
takes to attack from
3 to 2 when worn.

armor will
increase your
defense by 5.

A sword will
increase your
strength by 5.

A shield will
permanently
make fully
blocked attacks
deal 0 damage.
